# Privacy Policy — WebMakeover

_Last updated: July 2026_

WebMakeover is a browser extension that lets you adjust how a web page looks
(zoom, text size, line spacing, and colors) for more comfortable reading.

## The short version

**WebMakeover does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.**
It has no servers, no analytics, and no third‑party services. Everything happens
locally in your browser.

## What data is handled, and where it stays

- **Your display settings** (zoom level, text size, line spacing, chosen colors,
  and selected preset) are saved **locally on your device** using the browser's
  `chrome.storage` API, keyed by website so each site can remember your
  preferences. This data never leaves your computer and is never sent to us or
  anyone else.
- WebMakeover does **not** collect personally identifiable information, health
  information, financial information, authentication information, personal
  communications, location, browsing history, or user activity (clicks,
  keystrokes, mouse movement, etc.).

## Permissions and why they are used

- **activeTab** — apply your chosen appearance to the page you are currently
  viewing, only when you open the popup and make a change.
- **scripting** — inject the styling (font scaling, spacing, colors, zoom) into
  the active tab and cleanly remove it when you click Reset.
- **storage** — remember your per‑site settings locally so a page looks the same
  next time you visit.

None of these permissions are used to gather or transmit data. WebMakeover makes
no network requests.

## Data sharing

We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

## Removing your data

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored settings. You can also
clear them at any time from your browser's extension storage.
